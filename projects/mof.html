High-throughput screening of MOFs for carbon dioxide capture

In this work, my team and I are trying to utilize advanced quantum chemical modeling and atomistic machine learning techniques, particularly Grand Canonical Monte Carlo simulations and crystal graph convolutional neural networks, to discover high performance Metal-Organic Frameworks (MOFs) for carbon capture.
